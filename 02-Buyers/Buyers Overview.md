# Buyers Domain

Asian buyers discovering and purchasing European sporting goods through the platform.

## Portal

Route: `/buyer/*`
Accent: Green

## Pages

| Page | Route | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Dashboard | `/buyer/dashboard` | Overview, stats |
| Catalogue | `/buyer/catalogue` | Browse products |
| Quotes | `/buyer/quotes` | Quote requests |
| Orders | `/buyer/orders` | Order tracking |

## Capabilities

- Browse supplier product catalogue
- Request quotes
- Track orders
