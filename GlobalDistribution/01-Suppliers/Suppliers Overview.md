# Suppliers Domain

European sporting goods suppliers accessing the platform to manage their product catalogue and fulfil orders from Asian buyers.

## Portal

Route: `/supplier/*`
Accent: Blue

## Pages

| Page | Route | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Dashboard | `/supplier/dashboard` | Overview, stats |
| Products | `/supplier/products` | Product management |
| Orders | `/supplier/orders` | Order fulfillment |
| Upload | `/supplier/upload` | CSV product upload |
| Profile | `/supplier/profile` | Supplier profile |

## Capabilities

- Manage product listings
- View and fulfill incoming orders from buyers
- Upload product data via CSV
- Manage supplier profile
